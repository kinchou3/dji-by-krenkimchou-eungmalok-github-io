About DJI Cambodia

Your trusted partner for professional drone technology in Cambodia. #BY KREN-KIMCHOU

Our Story

Founded in 2018, DJI Cambodia is the official distributor of DJI products in the Kingdom of Cambodia. We bring the world's most advanced drone and camera technology to professionals and enthusiasts across the country.

From our humble beginnings as a small retail shop in Phnom Penh, we've grown to become Cambodia's leading authority on aerial photography and videography solutions.

Our Mission

We are committed to:

  • Providing the latest DJI technology to Cambodian creators
  • Offering comprehensive training and support
  • Promoting safe and responsible drone operation
  • Supporting local filmmakers, surveyors, and innovators
